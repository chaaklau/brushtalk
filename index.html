About Brushtalk Exchange

This visualization displays a collection of 15,335 brushtalk interactions between Chinese and Japanese intellectuals, annotated with multilingual properties.

Annotations:

  • Gloss: English Gloss
  • POS: Part of Speech tags
  • Mandarin: Pinyin pronunciation
  • Cantonese: Jyutping pronunciation
  • Korean: Romanization
  • Japanese: On'yomi and Kun'yomi readings
  • Stratum: Historical linguistic stratum (e.g., Classical, Modern, Qing)
